Try Keyn now!

Keyn has been launched for iOS and is available for testing on Android!

Since a few weeks, Keyn is available in the iOS AppStore. Make sure you tell anyone with an iPhone that they can now download the Keyn app.
Have you downloaded it via TestFlight? No problem! Keyn will still be available in TestFlight and you can keep using Keyn for free. Just don't hesitate to share your feedback with us :-)

For the Android users, there's also good news! Keyn is available for public beta testing. You can download the Keyn app on your Android smartphone by clicking this link: https://play.google.com/apps/testing/io.keyn.keyn.
Feel free to download Keyn and please let us know what you think!

LEF

Next week, Tuesday 19th of November, we're presenting Keyn in the finals of the startup competition LEF. On the main stage of Gebr. de Nobel in Leiden, we have ten minutes to convince the jury and the public why we are the best startup of Leiden. We'd really appreciate your attendance, so please sign up at https://www.heblef.nl/bezoeken!

And to make Keyn more accessible, we've translated the app, the extension and the website in Dutch. Make sure to check it out: switch to dutch. Also, check out our new video why you should use Keyn and how it works :-) We're also translating everything in Spanish with the help of our new developer Brandon, so stay tuned!

Do you have any questions? Feel free to drop us an email. We're happy to talk to you!
